Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how WK Aircond Service ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you interact with us via WhatsApp or our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through WhatsApp or our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, WhatsApp phone number, and any contact details you provide
  • Service Information: Your address, preferred appointment date/time, and description of your air conditioning issue
  • Conversation Data: Messages you send to our WhatsApp chatbot, including service inquiries, booking requests, and feedback
  • Device Information: General information about how you interact with our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our air conditioning services:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To schedule and confirm service appointments
  • To send service reminders or follow-up messages related to your booking
  • To improve our AI chatbot responses and service quality
  • To comply with legal obligations under Malaysian law

We do not use your information for marketing from third parties, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

3. WhatsApp & AI Chatbot

We use an AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot to handle customer inquiries automatically. When you message our WhatsApp business number:

  • Your messages are processed by our AI system (powered by Anthropic's Claude) to generate relevant responses
  • Conversation history is temporarily stored to maintain context within your chat session (up to 7 days)
  • Our WhatsApp integration is built on Meta's WhatsApp Business API and is subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Conversation data stored in our system is automatically deleted after 7 days of inactivity. Service booking records may be retained for up to 2 years for business and legal purposes.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your data only with:

  • Service technicians: Your address and appointment details shared with our technicians to complete your service
  • Technology providers: Cloud infrastructure and AI service providers who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities: When required by Malaysian law or lawful government request

6. Your Rights

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for us to process your data
  • Opt out of any communications from us

To exercise these rights, visit our Data Deletion page or contact us via WhatsApp.

7. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personal information and can be disabled in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
